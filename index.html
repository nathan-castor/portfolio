About

General Assembly Graduate!

Making projects that come to life and add value or pleasure to anyone's daily experience is what drives me. I love learning and developing skills that can turn an idea into a reality. I'm looking forward to producing excellent web applications and continuing to learn throughout my career.

WDI is a 12-week web development course at General Assembly. Throughout WDI I designed and built 5 websites/apps (each within 5 days!) and completed over 500 hours of instruction in a variety of programming languages, frameworks, libraries, and databases

Even as a graduate General Assembly is giving me the perfect environment to do what I love: learn and grow. I'm developing programming skills that I would otherwise have avoided (front end foundations) and expanding the skills I've been cultivating through self teaching (with the help of the internet). Combining these skills, I'm now able to create amazing games and websites that I can interact with in attractive representations of my creations.
